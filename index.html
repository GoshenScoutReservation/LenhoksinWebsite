What is Lenhok'sin?

Lenhok'sin High Adventure is Goshen Scout Reservation's nationally accredited high adventure program based out of Camp Baird. The program offers Scouts, Venturers, Explorers, and their adult leaders exciting high adventure opportunities they will enjoy and remember forever. The staff at Lenhok'sin will work with you to customize a trek based on your Crew's interests.

Whether you're looking for a backpacking trek in the forests of the Blue Ridge Mountains with adventures at our staffed outpostsor a canoeing trek on the James River Lenhok'sin is the place to Hit the Trail!

Treks

All treks have 5 days and 4 nights on the trail and/or river. Crews arrive at Camp Baird base camp on Sunday, with a Monday arrival option for LDS units, and depart on the following Saturday. We provide the food and have a variety of awards for your crew to achieve.

Lenhok'sin Trail Trek

Our standard trek choice, Lenhok'sin Trail are week-long backpacking treks that take you across the Goshen wilderness to amazing views and awesome activities at our staffed outposts. We will work with your crew to design an itinerary that fits the interests and abilities of your crew.

James River Canoe Trek

Take in one of Virginia's most scenic rivers on this self-guided trip. Paddle 61 miles of the historic Upper James River while you experience Class I and II rapids and the infamous Balcony Falls.

How Do We Get There?

Goshen Bus

Units may take advantage of the Goshen bus system chartered by our local council. All tickets are round trip and may be purchased through your designated unit registrar. Units are expected to provide at least one adult to ride on the bus with their campers. Buses depart and return to the Fairfax Government Center, but other locations may be available.

Driving

We do recommend driving as opposed to taking the Goshen chartered bus, especially for those that are going on the James River Trek, the Trail & River Combination Trek, or are planning on participating in the Caving outpost as part of the Lenhok’sin Trail Trek or the Trail & River Combination Trek.
